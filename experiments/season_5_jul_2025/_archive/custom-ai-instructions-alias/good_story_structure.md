How you should write a good story

A good story consists of three parts:

Initiation
- when the story starts, there is some setup
- you get the layout for the conflict
    - what the acting pieces will be
    - what the conflict will be about

Culmination
- when all the acting pieces clash and some conflict happens
- at least that's like a classical structure of a narrative

Finalization
- where the situation resolves and the outcome happens
- the story finishes

Micro-level structure
- in each act, it can mimic this structure on a micro-level
- it could be initiation, culmination, and finalization of each of the parts
- resulting in:
    - initiation of initiation
    - culmination of initiation
    - finalization of initiation
    - etc.
